Mission & Values

Why We Do What We Do

We believe travel is the greatest teacher. Every passport stamp is a lesson in empathy, every missed bus an adventure and every foreign meal a story worth telling.

🌍
Travel for Everyone

Whether you have $300 or $30,000, everyone deserves to see the world. We tailor experiences for backpackers and luxury seekers alike — no traveller left behind.

🤖
AI-First Planning

Our AI doesn't just suggest places — it understands your budget, travel style, visa situation and preferences to build itineraries that genuinely fit your life.

🔒
Trust & Transparency

No hidden fees. No fake reviews. Every price shown is real, every destination reviewed by real travellers, and every booking is 100% secure.

🌱
Responsible Travel

We champion eco-friendly stays, local guides and cultural sensitivity. 1% of every booking goes to our Travel Earth Fund for sustainable tourism projects.

⚡
Relentless Innovation

Travel is changing fast. We ship new features every two weeks — from real-time visa trackers to group trip planners — always listening to our traveller community.

❤️
Community First

Our 50K+ traveller community shares tips, travel hacks and honest reviews. Real people, real trips — not influencers on sponsored stays.

Our Journey

From Idea to Global Platform

Every great journey starts with a single step.

✈️
The Spark

Harshit Soni quits his job and backpacks 47 countries. Realises that great travel planning is a superpower not everyone has.

The
Idea
💡
Wanderlust Founded

Team of 3 founded in Uttar Pradesh. MVP launched — 10 countries, basic AI chatbot and a booking form.

🚀
100 Countries Milestone

Expanded to 100 destinations. First 5,000 users. Featured in TechCrunch India and raised $1M seed round.
