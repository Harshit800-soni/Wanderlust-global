Getting Started

New to Wanderlust Global?

Follow these steps to plan and book your first trip in minutes.

💡
Pro tip: Use our AI chatbot (bottom-right globe button on the home page) to get a personalised itinerary for any country within seconds — just tell it your budget and dates!
1
Create Your Account

Click "Sign Up" in the top navbar. Enter your name, email and password. Verify your email within 24 hours to unlock all features.

2
Search a Destination

Use the hero search bar on the home page or browse by continent using the filter bar. Type any country, city or region.

3
Use the AI Planner

Open the AI chatbot and type your trip preferences — e.g. "7 days in Japan under $1,500." It will generate a full day-by-day itinerary.

4
Book Your Trip

Click "Book This Trip" on any destination card. Fill in your details, pick travel dates on the calendar, and confirm your booking securely.

5
Check Visa Requirements

Each country card shows visa type (Free, eVisa, Visa on Arrival, etc.). Ask the AI chatbot for specific visa guidance for your passport.

6
Manage Your Bookings

Log in and go to "My Trips" in the navbar to view, modify or cancel any booking. Confirmation emails are sent instantly after booking.

✅
Need more help? Browse our FAQs or contact our support team — we reply within 2 hours on weekdays.

Common Questions

✈ Bookings & Payments
How do I book a trip on Wanderlust Global? +

Browse destinations on our home page, click "Book This Trip" on any country card, fill in your personal details, select your travel dates using the calendar, choose number of travellers and trip style, then confirm. You'll receive a booking confirmation email instantly.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept all major payment methods including:

  • Credit & Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Razorpay (India) and Stripe (International)
  • EMI options for bookings above ₹10,000
Is my payment information secure? +

Yes, absolutely. All payments are processed through 256-bit SSL encrypted connections. We never store your card details on our servers. Our payment partners (Razorpay & Stripe) are PCI-DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security.

Can I modify my booking after confirming? +

Yes! Log in to your account and go to "My Trips" in the navbar. You can modify dates, number of travellers and trip style up to 48 hours before your trip start date. Changes may be subject to availability and price differences.

Are the prices shown per person or total? +

All prices displayed on destination cards are per person (starting from price). The full total — including all travellers and applicable taxes — is shown in the booking modal's price estimate section before you confirm payment. No hidden fees.

🤖 AI Trip Planner
How does the AI trip planner work? +

Our AI chatbot (the globe 🌍 button, bottom-right) is powered by a large language model trained on travel data for all 195 countries. Simply type your query — like "Plan 10 days in Thailand for ₹50,000" — and it generates a personalised day-by-day itinerary, budget breakdown, hotel suggestions and tips.

Is the AI trip planner free to use? +

Yes — the AI chatbot is completely free for all users, including without an account. Registered users get additional features like saving AI-generated itineraries to their profile and exporting them as PDFs.

Can the AI help with visa information? +

Yes! Ask the AI "Visa-free countries for Indian passport" or "Do I need a visa for Japan from UAE?" and it will provide up-to-date visa guidance. However, we always recommend verifying critical visa information at the official embassy website before booking, as policies can change.

💰 Cancellations & Refunds
What is the cancellation policy? +

Our standard cancellation policy:

  • 30+ days before trip: 100% refund
  • 14-29 days before trip: 75% refund
  • 7-13 days before trip: 50% refund
  • Under 7 days: Non-refundable (travel credit offered)

Some partner-specific bookings may have different policies — always check the individual booking terms.

How long do refunds take? +

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method. UPI refunds typically arrive within 1-2 days. Credit card refunds may take up to 10 business days depending on your bank. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is initiated.

What if the trip is cancelled by Wanderlust? +

If we cancel your booking for any reason (partner unavailability, natural disaster, etc.), you will receive a 100% full refund within 3 business days, plus a 10% discount voucher for your next booking as an apology. You may also opt for travel credit instead.

👤 Account & Technical
How do I reset my password? +

Click "Login" in the navbar, then select "Forgot Password?" on the login modal. Enter your registered email address and we'll send a password reset link within 2 minutes. The link expires after 1 hour for security.

Can I use Wanderlust on my phone? +

Yes! Wanderlust Global is fully responsive and works on all devices — mobiles, tablets and desktops. Our mobile web experience is optimised for touch. A dedicated iOS and Android app is currently in development and will be available in late 2026.

How do I delete my account? +

To delete your account, go to Profile Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your personal data will be permanently erased within 30 days in accordance with GDPR and India's DPDP Act. Any pending bookings must be cancelled first. This action cannot be undone.

1Information We Collect

When you use Wanderlust Global, we collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address and password when you register.
  • Booking Details: Travel dates, destination, number of travellers, payment information and special requests.
  • Profile Data: Optional information such as profile photo, travel preferences and saved trips.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, time spent on pages, clicks and interactions.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system and device identifiers.
  • AI Chat Logs: Conversations with our AI trip planner to improve recommendations (anonymised after 90 days).

2How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process your bookings, payments and confirmations
  • To personalise AI trip recommendations based on your travel history and preferences
  • To send booking confirmations, receipts and important travel updates
  • To improve our platform through analytics and user feedback
  • To send promotional emails (only with your explicit consent — you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal obligations

We never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your data is used solely to improve your experience on Wanderlust Global.

3How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in limited circumstances:

  • Booking Partners: Hotels, tour operators and travel service providers receive only the information necessary to fulfil your booking (name, dates, traveller count).
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay and Stripe receive payment details to process transactions. They operate under their own privacy policies.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), email delivery (SendGrid) and analytics (Google Analytics) — all bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order or government authority.

We require all third parties to respect the security of your data and to treat it in accordance with applicable law.

4Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login sessions and booking flow. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site (Google Analytics). You can opt out.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme (dark/light mode) and language settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used only with your consent to show relevant travel ads on other platforms.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings at any time. Disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features.

5Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Payment data is processed by PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment partners
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing by third-party firms
  • Employee access to personal data is strictly limited on a need-to-know basis

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

6Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@wanderlust-global.com or use the Contact Support form. We will respond within 30 days.

7Children's Privacy

Wanderlust Global is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@wanderlust-global.com and we will delete such information from our systems.

8Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns or complaints about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer:

  • Email: privacy@wanderlust-global.com
  • Email: wanderlust@travel.com
  • Address: Wanderlust Global Pvt. Ltd., 42 Tech Park, Mumbai 400051, India
  • Response time: Within 30 days of receiving your request

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by email and update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Refund Policy

Clear and fair — we believe you should know exactly what to expect before you book.

📅 Effective: 1 April 2026

1Standard Cancellation Tiers

Our refund amount depends on how far in advance you cancel:

  • 30+ days before trip start: 100% full refund to original payment method
  • 14-29 days before trip: 75% refund
  • 7-13 days before trip: 50% refund
  • 2-6 days before trip: 25% refund or travel credit
  • Under 48 hours / no-show: Non-refundable. Travel credit may be offered at our discretion.

2Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • UPI / Net Banking: 1-3 business days
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5-10 business days (depends on issuing bank)
  • Wanderlust Travel Credit: Instant — credited to your account immediately

3Platform-Initiated Cancellations

If Wanderlust Global cancels your booking for any reason (including partner unavailability, natural disasters, or force majeure events), you will receive a 100% full refund within 3 business days, plus a 10% discount voucher for your next booking.

4How to Request a Refund

To cancel a booking and request a refund:

  1. Log in to your Wanderlust account
  2. Go to "My Trips" in the navigation bar
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm your reason
  5. You'll receive a refund confirmation email within 1 hour

Alternatively, email wanderlust@travel.com or use the Contact Support form.
